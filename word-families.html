Word Families

To understand Word Families, one needs to familiarize oneself with the Plastic Language Concept.

Plastic Language Concept

The Plastic Language Concept is where Words can alternate between Filters. The Dynamic Filter Logic Words has the Filters Gerunds and Logic Words. Gerunds can alternate between the Dynamic Filter Nouns, Dynamic Filter Logic Words, and Dynamic Filter Verbs.

Example: The word runner is a Noun when in the Dynamic Filter Nouns (Nouns > Nouns). Runner is a Logic Word when in the Filter Gerunds (Logic Words > Gerunds) and runner is a Verb when in the Dynamic Filter Verbs (Verbs > Gerunds).

Dynamic Filters

Dynamic Filter Logic Words

Dynamic Filter Logic Words has two Filters:

  • Logic Words
  • Gerunds

Dynamic Filter Nouns

Dynamic Filter Nouns has six Filters:

  • Nouns
  • Co-Nouns
  • Propper Noun
  • Persona Noun
  • I Object
  • Adjective

Dynamic Filter Verbs

Dynamic Filter Verbs has two Filters:

  • Verbs
  • Gerunds

Indicator Words

Miscellaneous, Dynamic Filter Indicator Words are not in the Plastic Language Concept.

Indicator Words has two Filters:

  • Portal Words
  • Finite Words

Gerunds and Word Roles

When a Noun is used as a Logic Word, the Noun is a Gerund. Gerunds are Logic Words as Gerunds are in the Logic Word Dynamic Filter, meaning a Noun can be used as a Logic Word. When a Noun is used as a Logic Word, the Noun is called a Gerund.

A Noun can also be used as a Verb and is called a Gerund as well when a Noun is used as a Verb (Logic Word > Gerund and Verb > Gerund).

As all Nouns can be used as Gerunds, we do not have a Filter Gerunds in Nouns Dynamic Filter.

Not all Logic Words can be Nouns. Only Noun Words used in Filter Gerunds can be called Logic Words. In the example below, the words run, running, and ran are in the Word Family of runner. Runner can be a Noun, Logic Word or Verb. Runs, running and ran are Logic Words or Verbs depending on the Grammar of the sentence.

Logic Words can be Verbs. When a Noun is used as a Gerund, there are three possibilities for the Noun: Noun, Logic Word or Verb. This is a part of the Plastic Language Concept. When a Logic Word is not a Gerund, there are only two possibilities for the Logic Word. In this case, the Logic Word is either a Logic Word or a Verb. Noun, Logic Word and Verb or Logic Word Verb are the two possible formats in the Plastic Language Concept.

All Logic Words can be used as Verbs. Not all Logic Words can be used as Nouns. Logic Words are Experience Words, and the Experience of a Logic Word can be projected as a Verb. Verbs being Descriptive Words in the Language Manual.

Examples

Runner as Logic Word

runner   — Logic Word > Gerund
runs     — Logic Word > Logic Word
running  — Logic Word > Logic Word
ran      — Logic Word > Logic Word

Runner as Verb

runner   — Verb > Gerund
runs     — Verb > Verb
running  — Verb > Verb
ran      — Verb > Verb

Descriptions

Dynamic Filter Logic Words has the Filters Logic Words and Gerunds.

Dynamic Filter Verbs has the Filters Verbs and Gerunds.

The Description of runner can be Logic Word, Logic Word > Gerund or Gerund when the word runner is a Logic Word: Logic Word, Logic Word > Gerund or Gerund.

The Description of runner can be Verb, Verb > Gerund or Gerund when the word runner is a Verb Word: Verb, Verb > Gerund or Gerund.

The Description of runs, running and ran can be Logic Word or Logic Word > Logic Word, for each word individually.

The Description of runs, running and ran can be Verb or Verb > Verb, for each word individually. Runs, running and ran cannot be a Noun or a Gerund.
